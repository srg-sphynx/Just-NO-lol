🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Just NO lol ("the App") is a fun, open-source web application that generates random rejection reasons. We take your privacy seriously — even more seriously than we take rejection. This policy explains what data the App collects (spoiler: almost nothing).

1. Data We Collect

TL;DR

We collect zero personal data. No accounts. No emails. No tracking. No nonsense.

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • No names, emails, or contact information
  • No IP address logging by the App itself
  • No user accounts or authentication
  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No fingerprinting or device identification

2. Local Storage

The App uses your browser's localStorage to save your rejection history locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device — it is not sent to any server
  • Is fully under your control — you can clear it anytime using the "Clear All" button or your browser settings
  • Contains only: rejection text, funny quotes, and timestamps
  • Contains no personal data

3. Cookies

The App uses one cookie:

  • justno-consent — Stores whether you've acknowledged the cookie/storage consent banner. This is a simple true value with no personal data. It expires after 365 days.

No third-party cookies are set. No advertising cookies. No tracking cookies.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is a general-audience humor tool. It does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no personal data at all, this is inherently satisfied.

6. Your Rights

Since we store no personal data on our servers, there is nothing to request, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear your local history: Click "Clear All" in the app, or clear your browser's localStorage
  • Block cookies: Configure your browser to block cookies for this site
  • Disable localStorage: Your browser settings allow you to disable localStorage entirely

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Given that this app generates rejection reasons and nothing else, changes are unlikely. But hey, we're thorough.

8. Contact

Questions? Concerns? Existential dread about cookie policies? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.
